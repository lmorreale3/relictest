ℹ About

This explorer maps documented relics of the True Cross from approximately the 12th to mid-13th century. Data is drawn from a scholarly catalogue of attestations organized by decade. Use the date slider and filters to explore how, where, and in what form these relics were documented across medieval Europe and the Near East.
